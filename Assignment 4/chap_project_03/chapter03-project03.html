Mademoiselle Carolina Revière

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Carolina Revière was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Revière family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil and Canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Musee de Louvre, Paris
